<h1>Smidle</h1>
<h2>Smite-Inspired Word Guessing Game</h2>

<h3>Overview</h3>
<p>Smidle is a captivating word-guessing game inspired by Wordle, designed for Smite enthusiasts. Players guess the names of mythological gods from Smite, testing their knowledge and passion for the game.</p>

<h3>Launch Success</h3>
<p>The game launched to great success, attracting <strong style="color: #ffd700;">20,000 visitors</strong> on its first day, demonstrating the strong appeal to the Smite community.</p>

<h3>Technical Implementation</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Frontend:</strong> Svelte for a smooth, responsive user experience</li>
<li><strong>Backend:</strong> Java Spring Boot for robust server-side logic</li>
<li><strong>Hosting:</strong> Initially hosted on home server, then migrated to cloud infrastructure</li>
<li><strong>Performance:</strong> Handled high traffic with no problems during viral launch</li>
</ul>

<h3>Key Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Wordle-style gameplay mechanics adapted for Smite gods</li>
<li>Knowledge-testing for mythological characters from the game</li>
<li>Responsive web design for desktop and mobile</li>
<li>Daily challenges for regular engagement</li>
</ul>

<h3>Project Highlights</h3>
<ul>
<li>20,000 visitors on launch day</li>
<li>Successful scaling from home server to cloud</li>
<li>Strong community engagement from Smite players</li>
<li>Smooth performance under viral traffic loads</li>
</ul>
